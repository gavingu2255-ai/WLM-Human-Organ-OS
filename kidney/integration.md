# Kidney — System Integration

The kidney interfaces with the whole-body OS through hemodynamics, endocrine
signaling, acid–base balance, and immune boundaries.

## Integration Channels
- Blood flow coupling (renal artery → renal vein)
- Endocrine feedback (RAAS, ADH, PTH)
- Electrolyte and osmolarity stabilization
- Acid–base buffering
- Immune invisibility and anti-fouling surfaces

## Structural Role
The kidney acts as a system-level stabilizer, maintaining internal environment
coherence across time, load, and physiological variability.
