# Appendix I — Engineering and Clinical Translation Pathway

## Engineering Phases
1. Simulation and structural validation  
2. Microfluidic MVP  
3. Integrated module prototype  
4. Large-animal testing  
5. Human early feasibility trials  
6. Clinical-grade device development  

## Regulatory Considerations
All clinical use requires independent regulatory approval, including safety
testing, biocompatibility, and long-term stability assessments.
