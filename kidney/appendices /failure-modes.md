# Appendix H — Failure Modes and Stability Conditions

## Structural Failure Modes
- +1 overload → excessive filtration pressure
- –1 collapse → insufficient gradient formation
- +2 drift → uncontrolled solute injection
- –2 breakdown → loss of countercurrent amplification
- +3 mis-signaling → incorrect systemic modulation
- –3 instability → oscillation or runaway feedback
- 0 discontinuity → flow disruption or leakage
- ±∞ breach → immune activation or fouling

## Stability Conditions
Each offset must remain within its structural envelope to maintain whole-organ
coherence. Stabilization is primarily governed by –3 and ±∞ layers.
